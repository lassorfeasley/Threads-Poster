How this is used

Enabled rules are injected into the caption-drafting prompt as authoritative style instructions (High-priority first), on top of the voice the AI already learns from your published captions.

Safety rules always hold regardless of what you write: the AI won't invent facts that aren't in the clip's transcript, it mentions the place, and it stays under the Threads character limit.
